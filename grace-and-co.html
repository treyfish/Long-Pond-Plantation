Grace & Co.

Hand-poured soy candles

the perfect candle

Grace & Co. began with a simple question: what does it take to make the perfect candle? For Stacey, the answer was American-grown soy wax, a carefully tested wick that burns long, even, and nearly soot-free — and scents that smell like home. Every candle is hand-poured in small batches right here at Long Pond, including fragrances inspired by the property itself.
