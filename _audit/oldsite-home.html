    ABOUT US

    Set on a 16 acre marsh front in beautiful Levy County lies Long Pond Plantation owned by Beauchamp Acres.

     

    Originally constructed in the early 60s this unique 3,000 sq ft home has all amenities you will need, equipped with an elite bridal & groom suite, tea room, kitchen and more. This adequate space is a photographers dream.  

    ​

     While touring our grounds here you will notice the mature live oaks covered in Spanish moss, overhanging across the event space making it a perfect balance of shade & light.

     

    Long Pond is the background of your event, sunsets fill the sky melting in pinks and yellows, making it a beautiful backdrop for your special day. 

     

    Words don't do justice to how beautiful it is here, We would love for you to see it for yourself.
