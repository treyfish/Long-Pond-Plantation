Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a tour?+

Book instantly through Square, or use the contact form — tell us your date and we'll set up a private walk of the grounds.

Can we do everything on site?+

Yes — your ceremony beneath the live oaks or inside the chandelier-lit White Barn, the reception in the barn, and getting ready in the historic home's bridal suite and guys lounge. One property, your whole day.

Do you host events besides weddings?+

Absolutely — corporate events, reunions, showers and luncheons in the tea room, and holiday parties in the historic home.

Do you offer decor and coordination?+

Yes — our in-family team at Slate & Pearl Events handles wedding design, day-of coordination, and a curated rental collection.

When is the best time for a sunset ceremony?+

Golden hour shifts with the season — in January, about 6:00 PM is just right, with the sun dipping low over the marsh. We'll help you time it perfectly for your date.

Is catering available?+

Light catering is available upon request, and our kitchen supports outside caterers — ask us about options when you book your tour.
